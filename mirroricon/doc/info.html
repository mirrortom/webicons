info information


  <i class="i-a-info"></i>

外侧圆圈是i标记,方点是:before,竖线是:after.绝对定位居中,上下位置设置圆点的top值和竖线的bottom值.

竖线上边和下边重叠了3个box-shadow(方点的),模仿了i字体下方竖线的形状.

完整样式


    .i-a-info
        border: .08em solid currentColor
        border-radius: 50%
    
        &:before, &:after
            background-color: currentColor
            left: 50%
            transform: translateX(-50%)

        &:before
            top: 15%
            width: .1em
            height: .1em
            box-shadow: -0.04em 0.17em,-0.05em .47em,0.05em .47em

        &:after
            top: 35%
            width: .1em
            height: .4em
