手机

phone 手机图标

<i class="i-a-phone"></i>

手机样子变化太大了,老式的苹果机下方有个home键.后来又是全面屏了.思考一番无结果,就体现一个home键.

机身:before,高宽比例1:0.6.边框圆角,上边有一点内阴影,产生立体感.

home键:after,盒模型高度为0.16em,下边圆角同机身,上边是直角. home键直径0.1em,用径向渐变实现.


完整样式

.i-a-phone
&:before, &:after
    left:50%
    transform: translate(-50%,-50%)

&:before
    top: 50%
    width: .6em
    height: 1em
    border: 1px solid
    border-radius: .06em
    box-shadow: inset 0 .04em

&:after
    top: 92%
    width: .6em
    height: .16em
    border-radius:0 0 .06em .06em
    background-image:radial-gradient(circle .05em,transparent 96%,currentColor)
